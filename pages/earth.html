Atmosphere

Magnetic Field

Generated by the motion of molten iron in the outer core. Protects Earth from solar and cosmic radiation.

  • Only known planet with life
  • Biosphere stretches from deep ocean trenches to upper atmosphere
  • Carbon, nitrogen, and water cycles sustain ecosystems

  • Over 8,000 satellites launched
  • Earth-observing satellites monitor weather, climate, land, oceans
  • International Space Station orbits ~400 km above Earth

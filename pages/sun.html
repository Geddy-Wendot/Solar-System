🌞 THE SUN

🌞 EVERYTHING KNOWN ABOUT THE SUN — UNABRIDGED

1. Identity & Classification

Names: Sun • Sol (Latin) • Helios (Greek).

Stellar Class: G2V main‑sequence star — yellowish surface, hydrogen‑fusing core.

Galactic Address: Orion Arm (Orion Spur) of the Milky Way.

Age: 4.603 ± 0.04 billion years — halfway through main‑sequence lifespan.

2. Physical Dimensions

Mass 1.988 × 10³⁰ kg (333 000 × Earth) Radius 696 340 km Diameter 1.3927 million km (109 × Earth) Volume 1.412 × 10¹⁸ km³ Mean Density 1.408 g/cm³

3. Composition

Core (mass fraction): H 34.6 % • He 63.4 % • Metals 2 % (C, O, Ne, Fe…)

Photosphere: H 74.9 % • He 23.8 % • Metals 1.3 %.

4. Internal Structure

  • Core (r ≤ 150 000 km) — 15 million °C; proton‑proton fusion produces 3.8 × 10²⁶ W.
  • Radiative Zone (0.15–0.70 R☉) — photons random‑walk for up to 170 000 years.
  • Convective Zone (0.70–1 R☉) — rising plasma granules 1000 km wide.

5. Atmospheric Layers

  • Photosphere ~5 772 K; sunspots ≈ 3 700 K.
  • Chromosphere 4 500 → 20 000 °C; spicules rocket upward.
  • Corona ≥ 1 million °C; mystery heating, visible in eclipses.

6. Magnetic Field & Solar Activity

11‑year cycle of sunspot minima/maxima; poles flip each cycle. Flares, prominences and CMEs can hurl 10¹⁶ g plasma at 1000 km/s.

7. Rotation & Motion

Equator rotates in 24.47 days, poles ~35 days. Orbits the Milky Way in ~230 million years at 828 000 km/h.

8. Energy Output & Radiation

Luminosity 3.828 × 10²⁶ W (≈1 trillion megaton bombs/s). Solar constant at Earth = 1 361 W/m².

9. Neutrinos

~6 × 10¹⁰ cm⁻² s⁻¹ pass through you. Detection confirms core fusion; B‑8 branch delivers up‑to‑15 MeV neutrinos.

10. Future Evolution

  • Red Giant in 5 Ga ➜ radius ≈ 1 AU.
  • Helium flash; He→C/O fusion.
  • Planetary nebula shell ejection.
  • White dwarf ➜ eventual black dwarf (trillions of yrs).

11. Spacecraft & Studies

  • SOHO 1995–present.
  • SDO Solar Dynamics Observatory 2010–present.
  • Parker Solar Probe 2018–present.
  • Solar Orbiter 2020–present.

12. Culture, History & Symbolism

Ra • Helios • Surya • Amaterasu • Inti — civilizations worshipped solar deities; solar calendars anchor timekeeping.
